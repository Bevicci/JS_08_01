Dietary Patterns




Definitions
Ketogenic High fats, moderate proteins, and very low carbohydrates
Mediterranean Plant-based eating plan with fish, poultry, whole grains, nuts, olive oil, and some dairy
Pescetarian Fish, as a main source of protein, vegetables and other plant-based foods
Vegetarian Do not eat meat or fish but may continue to eat eggs and dairy products
Vegan Adopting a plant-based diet and avoiding all foods that come from animals
